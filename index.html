    Data Transpose Lab

    Data Transpose Lab group is a team of faculty and students at the University of Adelaide. The Data Transpose Lab dedicated to exploring and advancing the field of data science and its applications across various domains. The lab group's focus areas include Natural Language Processing (NLP), Internet of Things (IoT), and Medical AI.
